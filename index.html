COS Trail Runs

A trail running guide for Colorado Springs residents - run rugged!

Calling the fit AND the fat!

Colorado Springs is a haven for runners of every level. From Olympic athletes to weekend warriors, the terrain offers a plethora of workouts and challenges for the active set of Little London.

This site offers a glimpse into the local trail running culture in hopes of whetting the appetite of anyone looking to up their game or join the ranks of the many who are out and about at all hours and in every kind of weather putting in time and miles.

It's worth noting that while many pro athletes come to train at altitude, much more of the running is done by amateurs who are looking to reap the benefits of an active lifestyle - that and working out in mother-nature's backyard! This site is mostly for us.

TRAILS:

Manitou Incline

It's seen the likes of Apollo Ono and, recently, Shaun White.

Cheyenne Canyon

From Seven Falls to Cripple Creek, this area is full of every grade of trail.

Pikes Peak

The Incline is a teaser for what this mammoth of a mountain run is like. People even run it more than once in a day!

Monument Valley Park

Fountain Creek and Bear Creek are just two of the many areas accessible from MVP. The more adventurous and long winded can travel further north or south of Colorado Springs if they so choose.

Red Rock Canyon

This little bit of paradise has so many branches that you can get lost, or at least feel that way.
