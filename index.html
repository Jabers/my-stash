If you are particularly savvy you would have pressed your gas pedal shortly after "2" in the countdown! That's right racers, we are serious about having fun, fun in a sort of sadistic, beam your enemy with a turtle shell way that is!

From mini boosts to drifting, we love the recklessness of Mario Kart Wii. From the advent of this raucous racing title from Nintendo, racers young and old alike have engaged in some of the most haphazard go kart competition ever.

This is just a fan site, visit their wiki-page for a more thorough look at Mario Kart on all platforms here.

On Your Mark…

This site aims to celebrate the innocent, yet rowdy and playfully vindictive aspect of Mario Kart Wii. To avoid clutter and confusion (we'll leave that to the racing) we focus on three areas of gameplay:

  1. Character Faves

    We each have our own favorite race time avatar - our go to driver. Some like the main guys, others live vicariously through their evil alter egos like Waluigi. We highlight some perennial faves as well as dark horses. Perhaps you may change your mind about who your favorite really is!

  2. Course Faves

    Each character has a course(s) that they should shine on. The truth is, each course has different playability as the settings are tweaked. For example, one advantaged racer on a particular course loses their edge when the item style is altered (curse you first place turtle shell!).

  3. Competition

    Their are tips and tricks to beating out the other racers, albeit few and fairly well known. However, competition still has a lure that is difficult to ignore, especially when your five year old is kicking booty chaw - your booty, that is!
