On Your Mark…

This site aims to celebrate the innocent, yet rowdy and playfully vindictive aspect of Mario Kart Wii. To avoid clutter and confusion(we'll leave that to the racing) we focus on three areas of gameplay:

  1. Character Faves

    2. We have our own race time avatar. Some like the main guys, others live vicariously through their evil alter egos like Waluigi. We highlight some perennial faves as well as dark horses. Perhaps you may change your mind about who your favorite really is!

    Course Faves

    Each character has a course(s) that they should shine on. The truth is, each course has different playability as the settings are tweaked. For example, one advantaged racer on a particular course loses their edge when the item style is altered (curse you first place turtle shell!).

  2. Competition

    3. Their are tips and tricks to beating out the other racers, albeit few and fairly well known. However, competition still has a lure that is difficult to ignore, especially when your five year old is kicking booty chaw - your booty, that is!
